About Zoolondia

Zoolondia Parc is a vibrant zoological and botanical sanctuary located in Brazzaville. Our mission is to inspire love and respect for wildlife through education, conservation, and unforgettable experiences.

Why Visit Us?

  • 🐘 Home to over 100 species from Africa and beyond
  • 🌿 Lush botanical gardens and shaded picnic areas
  • 🎓 Educational programs for schools and families
  • 🎟️ Affordable tickets and group discounts
